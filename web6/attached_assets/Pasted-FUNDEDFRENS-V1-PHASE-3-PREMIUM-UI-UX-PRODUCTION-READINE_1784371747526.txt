STRICT DEVELOPMENT RULES

Read these carefully.

1. Do NOT redesign the backend architecture.
2. Do NOT remove existing features.
3. Do NOT change database tables unless absolutely necessary and approved.
4. Do NOT change APIs unless required for stability.
5. Do NOT modify payment logic.
6. Do NOT modify challenge logic.
7. Do NOT modify referral logic.
8. Do NOT modify authentication.
9. Do NOT modify the business model.
10. Every visual improvement must preserve existing functionality.

If a UI change requires backend changes, stop and document why before proceeding.
